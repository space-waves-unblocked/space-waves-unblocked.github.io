How to Play Space Waves

  1. Load: Scroll to the Play section; the game starts automatically.
  2. Steer: Guide the arrow through neon tunnels without touching walls.
  3. Pace: Short levels, instant retries — learn the rhythm and tighten lines.
  4. Progress: Clear levels and chase faster times.

Tip: keep micro-adjustments light. Over-steering is the #1 crash cause.

Controls

  • ←/→ or A/D — turn
  • Mobile — swipe or hold to steer
  • R — quick retry (when supported)
  • Esc — pause/menu (when supported)

Note: exact keybinds may vary slightly by build.
